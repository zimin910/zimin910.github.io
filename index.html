Recipe #1: Creamy Shrimp Pasta Recipe

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 6 servings

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
fettuccini pasta 3/4 lb
large raw shrimp 1 Tbsp
olive oil 1 lb
onion(Medium) 1/2
unsalted butter 2 Tbsp
garlic clove 1
white wine 1 lb
large raw shrimp 1/3 cup
whipping cream 2 cups
shredded parmesan cheese 1/3 cup
whipping cream 2 cups
Sea salt, or to taste 1/2 tsp
black pepper, 1/4 tsp
Paprika 1/4 tsp
Parsley 1 tsp

Instructions

Step 1: Add 3/4 lb pasta to a pot of boiling water with 1 Tbsp salt and cook according to package instructions until al-dente. Drain without rinsing and set aside.

Step 2: While pasta is cooking, prepare the shrimp and sauce. Season shrimp with 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper and 1/4 tsp paprika. Place a large, non-stick pan over medium/high heat and add 1 Tbsp oil. Once oil is hot, add shrimp in a single layer and cook 2 min per side or just until cooked through and no longer translucent. Remove to a separate dish to prevent overcooking.

Step 3:In the same hot pan, add 2 Tbsp butter with finely chopped onion and sauté until soft and golden (3-5 mins), stirring often. Add minced garlic and sauté another minute until fragrant. Stir in 1/3 cup white wine and boil down until there is only 25% of the liquid left.

Step 4:Stir in 2 cups cream, bring to a light boil then simmer 2 min. Sprinkle sauce with 1/3 cup parmesan cheese and stir just until creamy and smooth. Let it come just to a simmer without boiling then turn off the heat and season sauce with more salt, pepper and paprika to taste.

Step 5:Stir in the drained pasta and cooked shrimp, tossing until noodles are well coated in sauce. Serve in warm pasta bowls with a generous sprinkle of finely chopped parsley, more parmesan cheese and some freshly cracked black pepper.

Recipe #3: Guacamole

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 4 servings

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Avocados 3
Lime 1
Salt 2 Tsp
Roma tomato 2
Diced onion 1/2 cup
Chopped cliantro 3 tbsp
Miced garlic 1 Tbsp
Cayenne pepper 1 pinch

Instructions

Step 1: Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.

Step 2: Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
